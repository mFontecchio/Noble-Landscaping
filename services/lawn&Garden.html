Lawn & Garden

Full service grounds maintenance with a smile.

Lawn & Yard Maintenance

Mowing using high-quality commercial mowers properly sized for the job. We will vary mowing patterns to reduce rutting of turf. We follow Florida's Best Management Practices to ensure optimum mowing height resulting in healthier and better-looking grass.

Edging and trimming: While regular hard edging is important, we are also careful not to expand planter beds by excessive or improper edging techniques.

Debris removal: All areas are blown-off after each service and all debris removed from the property.

Shrub and ornamental trimming: Regular trimming to ensure healthy plants while maintaining a neat & manicured look.

Pest Control

Having a lawn that's free of Ticks, Fleas and other annoying pests is one of the best ways to live more life outside. But pests can be more than just an annoyance. Common outdoor pests can transmit diseases and cause allergic reactions in both people and pets. Noble Landscaping can greatly reduce your exposure to pests and pest-associated diseases.

We use a certified specialist to apply disease and insect control products.
