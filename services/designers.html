Meet Our Designers

Naveen Kumar

Naveen graduated from University of South Florida with a BA in Community Design in 2015 and has been with Noble Landscaping ever since. She thrives on creating unique tailored landscapes, fit for a multitude of different tastes, all while staying under budget. She enjoys the outdoors, especially Trail Running with her dog, Coco.

Cathleen Seer

Cathleen is currently a student at the Art Institute of Tampa and has a passion for the outdoors. She loves creating modern, meditative spaces that allow one to escape the busy day to day. Cathleen hopes to one day open her own outdoor yoga studio, but for now, enjoys putting a little bit of happiness and serenity into people's landscapes.
