Meet Our Team

Jim Noble

Born in Brewster, NY. He received an Associates degree in Applied Science and Majored in Landscaping in 1980. He also holds his Best Management Practices Certification as issuedby the State of Florida. That same year he relocated to Florida. Jim joined an existing nursery on US 41. After two years at that location he branched out on his own into residential landscaping.

Jim started Noble Landscaping in 1990, with just a few plants along the side of the road where we are located today. Over the years the roadside stop has flourished into a business offering more than just a few plants. We offer a variety of services ranging from simple lawn and grounds maintenance to designing and decorating outdoor livingspaces and features.

Naveen Kumar

Naveen graduated from University of South Florida with a BA in Community Design in 2015 and has been with Noble Landscaping ever since. She thrives on creating unique tailored landscapes, fit for a multitude of different tastes, all while staying under budget. She enjoys the outdoors, especially Trail Running with her dog, Coco.

Cathleen Seer

Cathleen is currently a student at the Art Institute of Tampa and has a passion for the outdoors. She loves creating modern, meditative spaces that allow one to escape the busy day to day. Cathleen hopes to one day open her own outdoor yoga studio, but for now, enjoys putting a little bit of happiness and serenity into people's landscapes.

Eduardo Dutra

Eduardo has always wanted to work outside, he knew this ever since he was a little kid. As the Head Landscaper, he can do what he loves, while also ensuring beautiful projects are completed. He always enjoys the moment when the entire project comes together the best. When he is not outside at work, you can find him outside, either at the beach with his family, or exploring the woods near his home.
