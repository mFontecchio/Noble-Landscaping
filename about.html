About Us

Noble Landscaping has been serving residential and commercial clients in the Tampa Bay including area since 2002. We are recognized for our reliable, professional, affordable and high-quality work and our BBB rating proves it! Crowder's Landscaping is an accredited business with the Better Business Bureau with an A+ rating! We take pride in our work and our community.

Founder and Office

Noble Landscaping is a family owned business based on the belief that your needs are our number 1 priority. Our entire team is committed to this concept. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from referrals.

Born in Brewster, NY. He received an Associates degree in Applied Science and Majored in Landscaping in 1980. He also holds his Best Management Practices Certification as issuedby the State of Florida. That same year he relocated to Florida. Jim joined an existing nursery on US 41. After two years at that location he branched out on his own into residential landscaping.

Jim started Noble Landscaping in 1990, with just a few plants along the side of the road where we are located today. Over the years the roadside stop has flourished into a business offering more than just a few plants. We offer a variety of services ranging from simple lawn and grounds maintenance to designing and decorating outdoor livingspaces and features.
